How to buy

step 1: First you’ll need a ETH Wallet

Download and install a ETH-compatible wallet like Metamask. Set up your wallet by creating a new account, remember to keep your recovery phrase safe and secret.

step 2: Then you’ll need some ETH to swap for $ASTERINU

Buy ETH from an exchange or swap cross chain and send to your wallet

step 3: Now you're gonna need a DEX

Go to a ETH DEX, like Uniswap and connect your wallet by clicking the "Connect Wallet" button on the DEX. Always double check you're on the RIGHT SITE.

step 4: Time to Swap

Swap ETH for ASTERINU, review the transaction carefully and approve the swap in your wallet.

step 5: Now go frolic and celebrate having some ASTERINU in your wallet!
